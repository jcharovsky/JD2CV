# ATS CV Rules

- Use a text-based PDF, not a scanned image.
- Keep one column and a natural top-to-bottom reading order.
- Do not use tables, sidebars, text boxes, icons, images, charts, or decorative separators.
- Keep contact information in the body of the document, not only in headers or footers.
- Use standard section titles and simple role entries.
- Use common fonts and simple emphasis such as bold role titles.
- Use plain hyphen bullets.
- Verify parseable text with Python/PyPDF2 or another extractor.
- Confirm section order and tailored content from extracted text before finalizing.
